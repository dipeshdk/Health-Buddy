AMBULANCE PROTOCOL

1. Call phone number 7777/7666 in case of emergency only.

2. Give information to Health Center Reception regarding location.Phone number you are calling from and condition of the patient.

3. If possible, ask one person to stand roadside to direct the ambulance.

Ambulance will not be provided for OPD treatment and Investigation to the city.
